About

Anantay is a slow-living mountain retreat, created for those who seek stillness, warmth, and a deeper connection with nature. Rooted in the Himalayas, Anantay is not just a place to stay—it’s a feeling of arriving somewhere you didn’t know you were missing.

Our first home, Anantay – The Chalet, is now open in Shangarh, surrounded by pine forests, open meadows, and snow-kissed peaks. Built with wood, warmth, and intention, it offers cozy stays, soulful food, quiet mornings, and unhurried days—where time moves gently and life feels lighter.

Every detail at Anantay is designed to bring you closer to nature and yourself—organic, locally sourced food, thoughtfully crafted spaces, heartfelt hospitality, and experiences that celebrate simplicity.

Our second chapter, Anantay – The Courtyard in Tirthan, is opening soon. Nestled near the river and forests of Tirthan Valley, it will carry the same spirit of slow living, community, and conscious travel.

Anantay is an invitation—to pause, to breathe, and to belong.
